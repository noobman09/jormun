Skills

Black Hole

Sorcerers have this amazing skill called "Blackhole". This skill sucks demi-humans as well as regular monsters if they come close enough with the price of one blue gemstone. Targets that is caught are unable to move and cannot be damaged for 6 seconds or depends on the skill level. This skill cannot affect MVPs/Minis.

Summon Elemental

Sorcerers are masters of the four major elements; Fire, Water, Wind and Earth. Sorcerers summons these elements to extend their power depending on what property they're using at a cost of one blue gemstone. When summoning, Sorcerers can only have one element exist at a time. These power aided by the elements increases the elemental damage output of each property respectively.
